HOME SHOW EVENT

When: AUGUST 25TH, 2020

Where: VANCOUVER CONVENTION CENTER

About the Event

Our home show will be an evening full of showcases focused on our new products integreated into any room in your home! Whether you want to enhance your gaming and entertainment setup, or want to add a slight touch of colour to change the ambience of your room every now and then, this is the show for you! We will have exhibitions for bedrooms, entertainment rooms, and even kitchens available that showcase.

Special designers on our team and interior designers will be joining us for some presentations, including the one and only Chino the Shiba Inu! Our speakers are passionate about the use of smart home lighting and modernizing it to be able to change the atmosphere in your home. There will be presentations on how you can use each and every one of our products in the most creative way possible, as well as little tips and tricks that you might not have known! Especially with our new Level Bulb that includes one TRILLION colours, the possibilities are endless!
